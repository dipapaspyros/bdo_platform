Why BDO?
Vessel Operators
BigDataOcean provides data and tools that allow you to operate your fleet in a more efficient way.
Ship Owners
Acquire vessel operation data and find out how engine components behave under different conditions.
Public Authorities
Detect anomalies in vessel behaviour and enable automated alarms that suit your need in no time.
Researchers
Get access to scientific as well as commercial maritime data & run customizable big data analytics on them - with BigDataOcean, you don't have to be tech savvy to do all that.
Wave Energy Providers
Research potential locations for wave energy systems and estimate their potential energy output.
